### ✨ STYLE GUIDE
- Colors:
  - Background: `#0f0f10`
  - Text: `#e6eef7`
  - Accent: `#ffb84d`
  - Muted text: `#9aa0a6`
- Typography: “Inter”, sans-serif (weights 400–800)
- Layout: max-width 1100px container, generous whitespace
- Rounded corners, glassy surfaces, smooth scroll
- Buttons: rounded, accent-filled or outlined
- Hover states: subtle scale or brightness increase
- All sections separated with enough breathing space
